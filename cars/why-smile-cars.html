  • Why Smile Cars

We are at a stage of our lives where normal isn’t normal anymore. Now more than ever, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness. We pledge to our customers and employees that we stand behind this commitment with our “Smile Cars Safety Promise”.

Our cars are logo free

Yes, you read that right, our smile-cars logo is not present on the vehicles we have for rent. We follow this to help you enjoy the journey with "your" car rather than just "a" car.

Fun and convenience without having to buy and maintain a car

Think about it. You are renting a car which means you don't need to take care of it and repeatedly service it like you usually do for a car you've purchased. You can rent, have a fun journey and return the car.

A Variety of cars to choose from (including luxury cars)

From small to big, budget-friendly to luxury vehicles, we've got them all. Take your pick according to your trip.

Daily, weekly and monthly rate cards with exclusive price packages

Different offers for different packages. Who doesn't love discounts! If you spend more on a lengthier trip, it's ok because you'll be saving while renting the car.

Delivery of cars at prime pickup spots like Airport, Bus terminus

Arrive at your destination and hop into your vehicle instantly! No need to book a cab and get the car later.

Chauffeur assistance for long drives for an exclusive price

You focus on the trip and the experience of traveling in the vehicle while we handle the driving part.
